The Natinal e-Governance Plan of Indian Government seeks to lay the foundation and provide the impetus for long-term e-Governance within the country .The plan seeks to create the right goveranance and institutional mechanisms, set up the core infrastructure and policies and implements a number of mission mode projects at the center ,state and integrated services levels to create a citizens -centric and buissneess- centric environmental for goveranance.
